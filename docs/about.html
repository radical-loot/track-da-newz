About this site

This website collects and displays English-language news reports of violence committed against transgender, hijra, kinnar, aravani, thirunangai and other third-gender people in India, from 2019 onwards.

Articles are gathered from publicly available news sources and indexes, then filtered and summarised with the help of AI language models before being published here. Every entry links back to the original news article, and all reporting remains the work and property of the cited publication.

Independence

This is a personal venture, built and maintained by one person. It has no affiliation with any organisation — no NGO, political party, company, or government body — and receives no funding from anyone.

Accuracy

Because collection and summarisation are partly automated, the data may contain errors, duplicates, or omissions, and it is certainly not a complete record of all violence against trans people in India — only of what was reported in the English-language press and successfully found. Always refer to the original article for the full, authoritative account.

If you spot a mistake or a missing incident, corrections are welcome.
